Help us!

Help the Open Data community to identify the subjects of these paintings!


Wikidata, the free and open database worn by Wikimedia, lists more than 320,000 painted works. It is a fund of extraordinary richness, offering unprecedented possibilities to scholars and professionals of history, art history, and curious and enthusiastic.

To make the most of this tremendous source of wealth, it is imperative to describe the artworks and especially what is represented on them.
Just 15% of these works currently described on Wikidata.

This project aims to describe the other 280,000!

For this we need you, users who unknowingly use Wikidata's resources and richness on a daily basis when searching Wikipedia or Google.

To help us, it's easy: just describe what you see. If a picture depicts a man wearing a hat, enter "man" and then "hat". These two small actions will then allow us to correctly reference the work that you have indexed.

Thank you for your contribution !
