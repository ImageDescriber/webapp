The ImageDescription project aims to provide descriptive data about works painted in the project Wikidata.
All contributions and data produced by users, contributors and the project are irrevocably placed under CC-0 license.
The data produced by the users and contributors of the project ImageDescription will be pushed to the Wikidata project. Contributions will not be credited and will not be eligible to be credited to the Wikidata project.

The contribution to the ImageDescription project is open to anyone wishing to participate. Users' contributions are subject to the discretion of the persons administering the project or its hosting server. Contributions may be published, modified or deleted by those administering the project or hosting the project without notice.

The contribution to the ImageDescription project implies acceptance of the rules set out above.
